# Code of Conduct

## The Short Version

Be respectful. Be constructive. Help each other build something great.

## What We Expect

- **Be welcoming.** We want contributors of all experience levels and backgrounds to feel comfortable participating.
- **Be constructive.** Critique ideas, not people. Suggest improvements rather than just pointing out problems.
- **Be patient.** Not everyone has the same context. Explain things clearly when asked.
- **Respect differences.** We won't always agree. That's fine - disagree respectfully and move on.

## What We Won't Tolerate

- Harassment, insults, or personal attacks
- Discriminatory language or behavior
- Publishing others' private information
- Trolling or deliberately disruptive behavior
- Anything you wouldn't say to a colleague's face

## Scope

This applies to all project spaces - issues, pull requests, discussions, and any other communication channel associated with TeamHero.

## Enforcement

If someone violates this code of conduct, report it to the project maintainers at conduct@myteamhero.com. We'll review the situation and respond appropriately. Reports are kept confidential.

Consequences range from a warning to a permanent ban, depending on severity and history.
