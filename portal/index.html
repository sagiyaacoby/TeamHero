Before You Begin

TeamHero uses Claude AI to power intelligent agents that can perform actions on your behalf - including running commands, browsing the web, accessing files, and interacting with external services.

Your Claude subscription powers this tool. Your interactions with Claude through TeamHero are governed by Anthropic's Terms of Service. TeamHero is not affiliated with Anthropic.

AI agents can and will make mistakes. They may misinterpret instructions, take unintended actions, or produce incorrect results.

Before you proceed:
  • Do not give agents access to sensitive systems, credentials, or data unless you understand and accept the risks
  • Review agent plans before approving execution
  • Maintain backups of any data agents can access
  • You are responsible for all actions your agents take

    Privacy
    How TeamHero handles your data.
    Your data stays on your machine

    TeamHero runs entirely on your machine. No data is collected or transmitted to us. All agent data, tasks, credentials, and knowledge stay local. AI interactions go directly to your configured AI provider (e.g. Anthropic) under your own account and subscription.

    API Keys & Secrets
    Store API keys and tokens that your agents need. These are encrypted with AES-256 and injected as environment variables into agent sessions.
